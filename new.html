FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes, Frontend Mentor offers a significant number of free challenges. There are also premium challenges and designs available for subscribers.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Absolutely! Frontend Mentor encourages you to use completed projects in your portfolio to showcase your skills to potential employers.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

Frontend Mentor has a supportive community on Discord and forums where you can ask questions and get help from other developers.
